1h Strategy Controls

BUY when price is above the 1h open and the 15m Heiken-Ashi is green; SELL when below the open and HA red. A new opposite signal flips the position. That's the whole strategy — nothing else to tune.

When on, a new SELL closes a BUY (and vice versa). Turn OFF to hold to expiry.

Skip if the ask side can't absorb the stake (e.g. 20).
